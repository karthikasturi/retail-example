# Simple Ecommerce Retail Business Use Cases

This document outlines basic use cases for a demo ecommerce retail application.

## 1. User Registration & Login
- A customer can create an account using email and password.
- The customer can log in to access their account and order history.

## 2. Product Browsing
- Users can view a catalog of products with images, prices, and descriptions.
- Products can be filtered by category or searched by name.

## 3. Add to Cart
- Users can add products to their shopping cart.
- Cart contents are visible and editable before checkout.

## 4. Checkout & Order Placement
- Users can enter shipping details and place an order.
- Order confirmation is shown after successful payment.

## 5. Order History
- Users can view a list of their past orders and order details.

## 6. Admin: Product Management
- Admins can add, edit, or remove products from the catalog.
